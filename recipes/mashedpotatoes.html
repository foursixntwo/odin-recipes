Red Skin Mashed Potatoes

Declious take on traditional mashed potatoes

Ingredients

  • Red skin potatoes (3lbs)
  • Heavy whipping cream
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Chives
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Scrub potatoes
  2. Halve potatoes and rinse in a large pot
  3. Cover potatoes with cold water
  4. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for approximately 25 minutes or until fork-tender
  5. Add cream and butter, and lightly mash - don't overdo it
  6. Add chives and parmesan, and stir
