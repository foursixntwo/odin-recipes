Cast Iron Bison Burgers

Easy weeknight cast iron bison burgers

Ingredients

  • 1lb Ground Bison
  • Burger Buns
  • Butter
  • Onion
  • Sliced Tomato
  • Mustard

Steps

  1. Form patties slightly larger than the size of your buns as they will shrink some during cooking
  2. Preheat skillet on medium-high heat until just smoking
  3. Add the butter, then the patties right away
  4. Cook 3.5 minutes each side
  5. (Optional) Toast buns in separate pan until lightly golden brown
  6. Assemble with chosen, or suggested toppings
