Cast Iron Pork Chops

Easy weeknight cast iron pork chops

Ingredients

  • Pork chops(2)
  • Flour
  • Cayenne Pepper
  • Italian Seasoning
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Preheat skillet on medium-high heat until just smoking
  2. Mix flour and spices in shallow dish
  3. Pat dry pork chops and lightly dredge in flour mixture
  4. Add olive oil and butter to pan, then immediately add pork chops
  5. Cook approximately 5 minutes on each side until internal temperature of 140F is reached
  6. Let rest 3-5 minutes
